Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are needed to travel to Sikkim?

Indian nationals need a valid ID proof. Foreign tourists need a Protected Area Permit (PAP) issued from Gangtok.

What is the best time to visit North Sikkim?

March to June and October to December are ideal for clear skies and great views.
